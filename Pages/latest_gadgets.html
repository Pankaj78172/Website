India's defense forces are strengthening their airspace security with the introduction of cutting-edge air defense systems designed to safeguard against a variety of aerial threats. This is a crucial move as the country faces rising tensions in its borders, particularly with neighboring countries that have also been upgrading their defense arsenals. These air defense advancements come at a time when global powers are focusing on strengthening their defense mechanisms, especially to counter the rising threats posed by unmanned aerial vehicles (UAVs) and hypersonic missiles. India's proactive approach ensures that the country remains ahead of the curve, ready to tackle any future aerial challenges with efficiency and precision.
